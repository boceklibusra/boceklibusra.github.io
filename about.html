About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in geotechnical earthquake engineering with a background in civil engineering and architecture.

My research focuses on ground-motion modeling, DYFI intensity data, and spatial correlation of earthquake shaking.

I have experience in teaching and laboratory instruction in soil mechanics and foundation engineering.

My work combines research, teaching, and engineering practice.

  • Ground-motion modeling
  • DYFI data integration
  • Teaching and labs
  • Interdisciplinary research
